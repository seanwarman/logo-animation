Marketing
MARKETING

B2B marketers are facing a continually challenging landscape. Budget constraints, GDPR, data privacy, yet company growth remains imperative.

The challenges faced?

Challenge 1: Marketers need to find new ways to stay relevant and engage their audience, define and execute the most effective ABM programmes, and continue to generate high-quality sales qualified leads, ahead of their competitors.

Challenge 2: Traditional rules of targeting based on verticals, job roles, and company size are ineffective and outdated. As is generating content based on experience of their business and clients, and assumption of that industry.

How can we help?

Solution 1
Targeted focus to drive pipeline.

Whether its targeting the right accounts and individuals or segmenting the right demographics. Unrival can provide a highly focused, bespoke package to fit your marketing activity needs.
Maximise engagement with your accounts and enable stronger pipeline driving Account-Based Marketing programmes, at scale.

Solution 2
Aligning sales and marketing teams.

Unrival help marketers to work closely with sales in understanding the individuals within their accounts and how they consume content. Discover what to say to engage those individuals deeper, and which channels to push those messages out on in order to gain maximum engagement.

Solution 3
Insight driven ABM strategy

Marketers need actionable data-led insights to make informed decisions and drive their ABM strategy. Targeting the right decision makers with the right content, on the right channel, based on what they are saying and not assumption. This ensures when sales have a conversation the target company and individual are looking to address a business challenge that your company can solve.
